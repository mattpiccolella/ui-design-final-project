Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is DayTrippr designed for?
    2. DayTrippr is designed for the curious adventurer/tourist interested in exploring new locations. The site lists a collection of itineraries suggesting possible places to visit.
  2. How do I use DayTrippr?
    3. You can either be an adventurer and search through a collection of itineraries or create an itinerary for other explorers!
  3. How do I create an itinerary?
    4. Simply first look up places of interest. Then add it to a list. When the list is completed ,simply click create trip and your itinerary will be added to the collection.
  4. What does the route on Google Maps show?
    5. The Google Maps shows a suggested route of the places you can go according to the person who created the itinerary.
  5. Can I search through itineraries?
    6. Yes! Simply just type in what you are interested in, and our search bar will list itineraries with associated tags.
